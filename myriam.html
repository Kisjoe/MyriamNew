About Myriam Couture

Founded in 2020, Myriam Couture represents the pinnacle of leather craftsmanship. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans using only the finest ethically sourced leathers.

Our collections blend timeless elegance with contemporary design, creating leather goods that transcend fleeting trends to become lifelong companions.

From the selection of hides to the final stitch, every step in our process honors traditional techniques while embracing modern innovation.
